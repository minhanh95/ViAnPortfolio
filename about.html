About

Vian Nguyen

a multidisciplinary Art Director with a creative lineage rooted in Design with minors in spatial design and illustration. This foundation allows me to approach every project with a unique dual perspective: the technical rigor of an architect and the expressive soul of an artist. My work exists at the intersection of commercial precision and raw human emotion, bridging the gap between brand objectives and visceral storytelling.

As a creative developer, I navigate a diverse spectrum of scales-from high-impact commercial sets for major Vietnamese brands to avant-garde runway narratives and community-driven editorials. I specialize in translating complex concepts into immersive physical worlds, ensuring that whether the output is a global campaign or an intimate portrait, the result is a balanced, intentional, and evocative experience.
